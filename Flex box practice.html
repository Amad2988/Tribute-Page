Bruce Lee Tribute

Bruce Lee was one of the greatest martial artist's in the world. Lee was born in San Francisco, CA on November 27, 1940. He studied under Ip Man He rose to fame in the 1960s and 1970s in breakout roles. Some of his notable movies include "Fist of Fury" "Enter the Dragon" and "The Game of Death". He passed away on July 20, 1973. But his impact and lasting legacy still live on. Jeet-Kune Do a martial arts philosophy drawing from different combat disciplines paved the way for modern day mixed martial arts.
